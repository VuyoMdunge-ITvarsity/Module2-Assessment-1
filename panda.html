ELEPHANT

Pandas are bears that are native to China and thanks to films such as kung fu panda and their cuteness they have become extreamly recognisable and loved in the world.

Pandas are also recognisable due to their unique apperance, they do not look like any other Their faces are also remarkably round whereas most bears have more square faces.

Bamboo makes up the majority of their diet, though they are techniaclly omnivores as they occatonally eat small mammals and birds. Their diegestive system is specially modified to digest bamboo.

Their natural habitat is in the mountainous regions of China of central China primarily the regions of Sichuan, Shaanxi, and Gansu provinces.
